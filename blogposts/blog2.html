Unique Job Skills to name

When asked about your skillset in a job interview, you have 2 options - say something boring, like teamwork OR say something interesting, like couch flipping. Some really cool skills to name in my opinion are:

  • Writing
  • Sleight of hand (shows your hobbies)
  • Dog whispering
  • Driving a car in reverse for extended periods of time (you're prepared for the worst)
  • Extreme knowledge on zombie apocalpyse survival
